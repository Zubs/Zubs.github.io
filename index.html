Beyond the Web

Mainframe & Enterprise Computing

Alongside my work in modern web systems, I've developed a genuine fascination with mainframe computing — one of the most resilient and consequential platforms in the history of software. IBM Z systems underpin a huge portion of the world's banking, insurance, and healthcare infrastructure, and understanding them at a deep level feels like understanding computing itself.

I'm an IBM Z Student Ambassador, and serve as Secretary / Vice-President of the Mainframe Student Society at the University of Wolverhampton — working to grow awareness of enterprise computing among the next generation of engineers. I've also written and published an introduction to mainframe systems on Medium, covering the Workload Manager (WLM) — a component that manages millions of jobs daily with near-zero human intervention.
